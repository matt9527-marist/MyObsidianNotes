## Modern Business Environment 
Executives - responsible for the culture of the organization to accept project management and to ensure projects are successful. Determine strategy and future direction. 
High Speed - Products and services need to get to market faster than ever before; thus project  
management has to ensure the processes focus on value and strategic alignment to business goals.
Things can change in the market extremely quickly.
High Change - Businesses and political climate cause frequent changes that project management must accommodate and embrace.
